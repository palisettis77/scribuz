Our story

It started with a question.

We were fans too. We watched the same food creators, followed the same fitness coaches, laughed at the same comedians. And one day someone asked — "wouldn't it be incredible to actually meet them?"

Not at a chaotic meet-and-greet where you get 30 seconds and a blurry photo. A real experience. Intimate. Personal. Something you would remember.

"We didn't build ScriBuz for fans who want autographs. We built it for fans who want connection — real, human, in-person connection."

We launched in Hyderabad in 2026. Phase 1 is just the beginning. Every city in India will have its own ScriBuz community.
