FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost to book a creator?⌄
Each creator sets their own starting price, shown clearly on their profile. ScriBuz adds a 5% platform fee on top. No hidden charges — ever.
What kinds of events can I book a creator for?⌄
Birthdays, weddings, corporate events, house parties, workshops, college fests, brand launches, shop openings and more. Just describe your event in the booking request.
What happens if a creator cancels?⌄
You receive a full refund — no questions asked. Our support team will also help you find an alternative creator if needed.
Can I reschedule a booking?⌄
Yes. You can request a reschedule from your account dashboard. The creator will accept or decline the new date. If declined, your original date stays.
Is ScriBuz available outside Hyderabad?⌄
We launched in Hyderabad — Phase 1. More Indian cities are coming soon. Join the waitlist on our homepage to be first to know when we're live near you.
How do creators get paid?⌄
Creators request a UPI payout from their dashboard. Earnings are transferred within 24 hours of the experience completing. No waiting weeks for payment.
