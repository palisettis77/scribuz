Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Applies to all ScriBuz users

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1 Who We Are

ScriBuz is a creator booking platform operated from Hyderabad, India. For any privacy questions contact us at hello@scribuz.in.

2 What Data We Collect

DataWhy we collect it
Name, email, phoneTo create your account and contact you about bookings
CityTo show you relevant creators near you
Booking detailsTo process and manage your experiences
Payment infoProcessed by Razorpay — we never store card details
Chat messagesTo enable communication between fans and creators
Reviews & ratingsTo help fans make informed booking decisions

3 How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve the ScriBuz platform
  • To process bookings and payments securely
  • To send booking confirmations and updates via email or WhatsApp
  • To verify creator identities and maintain platform safety
  • To resolve disputes between fans and creators
  • To comply with Indian legal and tax requirements

4 Who We Share Data With

We only share your data with third parties when absolutely necessary:

  • Razorpay — to process payments securely
  • Supabase — our database provider, data stored in secure cloud
  • Zoho — to send transactional emails
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required by Indian law

We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third-party marketers.

5 Data Security

  • All passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords
  • All connections to ScriBuz use HTTPS encryption
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see your card details
  • Access to our database is restricted, logged and monitored

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Email us at hello@scribuz.in and we will respond within 7 days.

7 Cookies

ScriBuz uses minimal cookies — only what is necessary to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or share cookie data with third parties.

8 Data Retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we remove all personal data within 14 days. Booking records may be retained for legal compliance purposes only.

9 Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes we will notify you by email at least 7 days before they take effect. The latest version is always available at scribuz.in/fan/privacy.html.

Privacy questions?

We take every concern seriously and respond within 48 hours.
